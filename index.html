Who I am

I am a fully qualified teacher at a prestigious preparatory school just outside London. I currently teach predominantly French and Latin, and have previously taught History, Geography, and RS, as well as whole-class and individual lessons on 11+ and ISEB Pretest preparation.

I also coach football, hockey, rugby, and cricket, run a public speaking club, and organise a whole-school University Challenge–style quiz. I am a believer in lifelong learning, and am currently completing a Master's dissertation in Educational Leadership.

Private tutoring allows the personalised, 1-to-1 contact that classroom teaching cannot always provide. I tailor every lesson to the individual — identifying strengths, addressing weaknesses, and bringing enthusiasm to every session.

My philosophy

01

Exam-Focused Rigour

With deep knowledge of ISEB Common Entrance, 11+, and GCSE requirements, lessons are purposeful and target exactly what is needed to succeed under exam conditions.
